Views - Navigator View

GenJ offers several ways to navigate through your genealogical data. You can pick individuals and families in the Tree View, select entities in the Table View or browse properties in the Edit View and jump to other referenced entities. As a navigating tool the Navigator View allows you to follow relationships of individuals globally. Other views opened on the same Gedcom file will change their selection appropriately to match this.

The following picture shows what the Navigator View offers with its six buttons. At the top the name of the currently selected individual is displayed. The buttons below are arranged according to their functionality (from a hierarchical perspective).

  1. Up - Father
  2. Up - Mother
  3. Left - Older Sibling
  4. Center - Spouse
  5. Right - Younger Sibling
  6. Down - (1st) child
Every click on one of these buttons will navigate in the "according direction" (buttons like (e.g) Father will be disabled if no father for the current individual is defined).

How to close a genealogy

Each time I run Ancestris, my genealogy is well opened, but I have also the Kennedy genealogy running. How do I get rid of it? How do I close it, to have just my genealogy?

Put the focus on the genealogy you want to close. Then from the main menu, click on "File" - "Close". That's all. Easy. Next time you run Ancestris it won't be opened anymore.
Another way is to use the Gedcom explorer. Check on the left of the main window. You will see a kind of button wich is named GEDCOM explorer. Put the pointer of your mouse on it, it will open the explorer.



Right click on the name of the genealogy you want to close. Click on "Close".



That's all.

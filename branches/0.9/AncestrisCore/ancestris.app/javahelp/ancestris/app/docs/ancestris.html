ANCESTRIS - FIRST TIPS


Before starting your genealogy with Ancestris, and we know you are in a hurry to do so, we would like to give you differents tips that will help you to use Ancestris, and to enjoy its full potential.
This will help you to avoid making mistakes in the way you build your genealogy. Don't forget you have to follow the Gedcom specifications, and if you don't do that, you will take lots of time to fix your errors and rebuild your data. Ancestris is there to guide you and make your genealogy work a real pleasure.

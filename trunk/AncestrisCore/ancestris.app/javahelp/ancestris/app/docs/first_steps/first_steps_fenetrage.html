Window Management


With Ancestris you can organize your desktop to suit your work habits. You can choose the windows you wish to make visible, wherever and in whatever shape you prefer. All these changes are reversible, so do not hesitate to try.


All windows can show up as

  • frames (piled-up or side by side) (1)
  • tabs (their default look), clicking on their title brings them forward (2).
  • retractable windows against the right, left or bottom edge (3).
    • Simply hover the tab with the mouse to open it.
    • To close a window, hover over another window.
    • To dock the window, click on the "Pin" icon. It turns into a "Minimize window" icon you can click to revert the window to its initial state.



    or

  • floating (undocked) windows that can be placed anywhere on the screen. This may be convenient for some of them (Search Block, Help...)
    • Right click / last menu line.
    • To dock them again, right click on the tab / last menu line.

Opening and closing windows

Any window can be closed by left clicking on the cross on the right of the title.
To reopen it, a left click on the selected line in the Display menu will restore it to its previous position.


Moving windows

Maintain left click on the title while dragging, then release the mouse button to set the window. While dragging the window, you get a preview of its location and its shape (frame or tab) in red outline.


Window size

Clicking and dragging separation lines can change the window size. Each window can be displayed at its full frame.


All these options can be mixed: tab windows, retracted ones, a floating one... As you like!


If you wish to keep the same window location the next time you reopen Ancestris, save your choice.
Choose "Tools / Save display settings" in the main menu bar.

You can find explanations on window management in a video tutorial available on the Ancestris wiki site.
